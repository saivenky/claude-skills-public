---
name: science-backed
description: Use when recommendations should be grounded in scientific evidence — enforces peer-reviewed research first, credible sources as fallback
---

When making recommendations that depend on evidence:

1. **Prefer peer-reviewed research** — search for studies, systematic reviews, or meta-analyses using WebSearch
2. **Fallback to credible sources** when studies are absent or limited — authoritative guidelines, established organizations, expert consensus in the relevant field
3. **Flag the distinction** — never present a credible-source fallback as equivalent to peer-reviewed evidence
4. Acknowledge explicitly when evidence is mixed, actively debated, or insufficient to make a strong claim
