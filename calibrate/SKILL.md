description: Post-session retrospective that surfaces missteps and proposes targeted fixes to settings, skills, or memory.
---
Walk through this conversation and find every decision where the first attempt failed, needed correction, or drew pushback. For each: what was tried, what went wrong, what was needed.

Then propose specific fixes — memory updates, skill edits, or settings changes. Walk through each with me and implement what I approve.

Look hard. Don't rubber-stamp the session.
